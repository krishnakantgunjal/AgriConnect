Irrigation Systems
Types of Irrigation Systems

Explore different irrigation methods and their specific applications for various farming scenarios.

Drip Irrigation

Water-efficient system that delivers water directly to plant roots, minimizing waste.

Efficiency: 90-95%

Sprinkler Irrigation

Simulates rainfall by spraying water over crops through a network of pipes.

Efficiency: 75-85%

Furrow Irrigation

Traditional method where water flows through small trenches between crop rows.

Efficiency: 60-70%

Center Pivot Irrigation

Mechanized system that rotates around a pivot, covering circular fields.

Efficiency: 80-90%

  • Install rainwater harvesting systems

  • Regularly maintain and check for leaks
  • Consider drought-resistant crop varieties
